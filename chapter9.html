Usability testing

Any testing with anyone is better than no testing, and a bit of testing earlier is better than a lot of testing later. Test with three or four users each round, and test for more than one round to catch new problems. Use a screen recorder during user tests to easily share and review them later. It doesn't much matter whom you test with -- take anyone you can get, and grade on a curve Before designing your own site, test a live, comparable site to see what works and what doesn't.

Accessibility issues

If something confuses most people who use your site, it's almost certain to confuse users with accessibility issues. Put your web site content in the order that a screen reader should read it and use CSS to adjust its position. Put a "Skip to Main Content" link at the beginning of each page.so it is easily for those people who need something from your web site
